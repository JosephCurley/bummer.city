Joseph Curley is a Brooklyn based web developer and artist currently working as a member of the digital team at the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

He has a background in design, printmaking and photography, and uses this website to document his various creative projects. Find him skateboarding or biking around Bushwick, at a show, or in a coffeeshop drawing or reading comics.
